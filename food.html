Rujak Cingur

Rujak cingur is one of the traditional dishes unique to Surabaya that is well-known. This dish consists of main ingredients such as slices of cingur (cooked cow snout), vegetables like bean sprouts and water spinach, as well as slices of fruits such as young mango, pineapple, and cucumber.

All these ingredients are then drizzled with a ground sauce made from peanuts, petis (fermented shrimp paste), and slices of young banana that are unique. The thick sauce has a spicy and savory flavor.
