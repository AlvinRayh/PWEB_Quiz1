Monument Kapal Selam

Monumen Kapal Selam (Monkasel), located in the heart of Surabaya, is one of the city’s most iconic and unique tourist attractions. This monument is an actual retired submarine, KRI Pasopati 410, a Whiskey-class Soviet submarine that served in the Indonesian Navy. The submarine was transformed into a museum to commemorate Indonesia’s maritime history and to honor the crew who served aboard during its active years.

Visitors to Monumen Kapal Selam can explore the submarine’s interior, experiencing firsthand what life was like for the crew members. The cramped quarters, control rooms, torpedo launchers, and living spaces have been preserved, offering a rare glimpse into the operational conditions of the vessel. Information panels and displays throughout the submarine provide historical context, detailing the submarine’s missions and its role in protecting Indonesia’s waters during the mid-20th century.
